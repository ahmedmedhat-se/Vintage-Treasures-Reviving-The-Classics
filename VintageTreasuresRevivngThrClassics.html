Dimensions refer to the physical aspects of the game, such as terrain,
objects, and characters. Video game dimensions are important in

Controls

2D games often have simpler controls, with players only needing to
move left and right, jump and attack. On the other hand,
3D games require more complex controls to move the character
in three dimensions, which may take some getting used to for new players.
